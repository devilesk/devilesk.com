--- title: Interactive Map Tower Vision and Share Links Update description: > Interactive dota 2 map updated tower vision with fog of war and shareable links. created: !!timestamp '2015-05-19 11:00:00' tags: - Interactive Dota Map - update post_images: images/blog/interactive-map-tower-vision-update/ --- {% mark image -%} ![Interactive Map Tower Vision Update]([[!!images/blog/interactive-map-tower-vision-update/header.jpg]]) {%- endmark %} {% mark excerpt -%} I've updated the [dota 2 interactive map](/dota2/apps/interactivemap2) to show tower vision with fog of war and shareable links. {%- endmark %} Whenever you turn on layers or place wards, the URL will update and you can use that link to share the current map. Some examples: * [http://devilesk.com/dota2/apps/interactivemap2/?NeutralCampsSpawnBoxes=true](http://devilesk.com/dota2/apps/interactivemap2/?NeutralCampsSpawnBoxes=true) * [http://devilesk.com/dota2/apps/interactivemap2/?observer=-1807,1622;2563,-1944](http://devilesk.com/dota2/apps/interactivemap2/?observer=-1807,1622;2563,-1944) You can even share a map with trees cut down. (Trees can be cut by turning on the trees layer and clicking a tree marker.) * [http://devilesk.com/dota2/apps/interactivemap2/?Trees=true&cut_trees=384,832;448,960;512,832;576,960;640,832;768,896;896,896](http://devilesk.com/dota2/apps/interactivemap2/?Trees=true&cut_trees=384,832;448,960;512,832;576,960;640,832;768,896;896,896) Tower vision with fog can be seen by turning on the Towers layer and then clicking a tower. ![Tower Ranges]([[!!images/blog/interactive-map-tower-vision-update/tower_ranges.jpg]]) More updates to come, like tower night vision with fog and being able to edit the vision radius.
