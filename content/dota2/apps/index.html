Ability Matching

Click and drag ability icons into the right slot.

Ability Trivia

Answer 25 randomly generated questions about ability property values.

Ability Name Trivia

Name randomly selected abilities.

Hero Name Trivia

Name randomly selected heroes.

Item Name Trivia

Name randomly selected items.

Item Cost Trivia

Choose the correct cost of randomly selected items.

Drawable Map

A Dota 2 map you can draw on. Choose your brush color and size, place or paint hero icons, upload and share your drawing to imgur, or save it and continue drawing later.

Interactive Map

A high resolution Dota 2 map you can interact with. There are several overlays you can turn on and off including building locations, jungle camp locations, ward locations, and more.

Interactive Map 2

A high resolution Dota 2 map you can interact with. View neutral camp spawn box sizes, place and see ward sight ranges, measure distances, and see tower sight ranges.

Hero Calculator

A hero calculator that takes into account level, items, skills, and stats. Choose hero level, items, and skills and see detailed information about a hero's stats.

Hero Selection Trainer

A timed game to help you learn where each hero is on the hero selection screen.

Item Scramble

A puzzle game solved by dragging together all the given component items to form upgrade items.
