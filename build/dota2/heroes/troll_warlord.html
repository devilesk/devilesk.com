Berserker's Rage
Ability: No Target, Toggle
While active, Troll Warlord swaps from using ranged to melee attacks. Melee attacks have a chance to bash, dealing bonus damage and stunning the target. Berserker's Rage also grants additional health, movement speed, armor, and increased attack speed.

Whirling Axes has different functionality while Berserker's Rage is active.
